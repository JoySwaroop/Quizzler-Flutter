## What is this App All About
This is an interactive True/False quiz app designed to challenge your knowledge on various topics. With each question, simply choose whether the statement is true or false. After each response, you'll immediately know if your answer was correct. At the end of the quiz, your final score will be displayed, allowing you to track your progress and improve over time. Perfect for quick learning and fun!
